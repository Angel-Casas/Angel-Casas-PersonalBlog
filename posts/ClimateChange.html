Effects of CO2, positive and negative.

Biological lifeforms throughout history have reduced the amount of co2 in the planet and increased the amount of oxygen, allowing more complex lifeforms to benefit from this increasing richness in oxygen.

Carbon is associated with most lifeforms and it's effect in the cycle of life is crucial. This means a big variation in the amount of co2 in the atmosphere can have unpredictable consequences in many lifeforms on earth.

An increase in co2 in the atmosphere increases the absorption by green biomass, but also in the seas and lakes therefor increasing its acidity.
